Indian Taco

By Shreya

Hey there! Hope you guys are doing great!! I am back with another interesting post!

Ever thought of amazing snacks from leftover rice, dal and curry? So are one amazing snacks recipes for you.

preparation time -20mins

Ingredients-

*Leftover roti
*Sliced onions-1
*Grated carrot-half cup
*Chopped Capsicum
*Chilli powder
*Garam masala
*Black pepper powder
*Tomato ketchup/pizza sauce
*Grated/slice cheese
*For Tikki-
*Leftover rice, dal/ curry(only curry can also be used Or rice with curry) -1 bowl
*Boiled potatoes-3
*Chopped onions-2
*Chaat masala
*Red chilli powder
*Green chillis
*Garam masala
*maida
*Cornflour
*Water
*Bread crumbs

Procedure

To prepare tikki follow the same procedure as that of golden fried balls just give a shape like that of tikki rather than balls. Fry the sliced onions, carrot, capsicum. Add garam masala, salt, red chilli powder. Take the roti and spread ketchup/pizza sauce in it. Place 2 tikkis in one half of roti. Add the fried veggies on the top of it. Add the grated cheese on it and sprinkle some black pepper. Fold the roti in a taco shape. Toast in a pan with little oil. Serve hot!
