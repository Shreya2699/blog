Cheesy Macaroni Pasta

By Shreya

Ready for some yummy Ozzy cheesy recipes? There you go.....a yummy cheesy comfort recipes to soothe your mood any time. It especially feels great during chilled winter days or rainy days. So in this monsoon season get ready for some drooling cheesy recipes-Cheese macaroni pasta.

ingredients-

*cheddar cheese
*half cup milk
*1 n half cup macaroni pasta
*sweet corn
*black pepper
*oregano

procedure-

Boil the pasta in water put a little oil in the water to boil. Stain the pasta from water. Add milk, cheddar cheese/processed cheese, corn into the pasta and boil it. Season it with oregano and black pepper. Serve hot!
